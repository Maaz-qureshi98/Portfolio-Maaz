    Google Scholar Profile

    Developed a full-scale distributed volumetric SLAM pipeline with software development with ORB-SLAM3 (front-end), TSDF–Octree fusion (middle-end), 4D mmWave radar + RGB-D perception, and 5G offloading for real-time multi-robot mapping under occlusions and degraded visual conditions.

    Designed an automated collaborative-robot framework using OMPL RRT-Connect and quaternion kinematics for high-precision mmWave antenna radiation pattern measurement in 3D space.
