    About Me

    I am a robotics & AI research engineer with a passion for building intelligent systems that help people work safer, faster, and better. My work spans multi-robot SLAM, collaborative manipulation, mmWave radar automation, distributed 5G-connected robotics, and high-fidelity computational physics of biological swimmers.

    I have developed large-scale robotics systems integrating perception, communication, motion planning, and simulation across academic and industry labs — including emerging technologies in multi-agent autonomy and real-time robotic sensing.

    During my M.A.Sc. studies, I worked at the University of Waterloo with Dr. William Melek and Dr. George Shaker, and collaborated with Apple, UW RoboHub, Keysight, and Rogers on connected robotics systems.
