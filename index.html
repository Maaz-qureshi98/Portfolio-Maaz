    Robotics & AI Research Software Engineer

    I am a mechatronics engineer with a passion for building intelligent robots to work better. My research work spans multi-robot SLAM autonomy, manipulator motion planning, mmWave 4D radar & RGB-D sensor fusion, distributed 5G-connected robotics, and computational intelligence.

    During my M.A.Sc (applied research) studies, I spent time at the University of Waterloo (with Dr. William Melek and Dr. George Shaker), Apple Inc., UW Robohub, Keysight Technologies Inc., and Rogers Communications Canada Inc., where I worked on connected robotics technology.

    Earlier in my life, I developed a passion for swarm robotics, which led to winning 3rd place in Best Engineering Science Final Year Project during my B.Sc. Later, I was selected to supervise a NUST University Islamabad team for the Aerotech IMECHE United Kingdom 2022 competition, developing a hexacopter for a locust-swarm autonomy mission.
