    About Me

    I am an AI/ML and robotics research engineer focused on building intelligent systems for real-world autonomy. My work spans multi-robot SLAM, collaborative manipulation, mmWave radar perception and automation, distributed 5G-connected robotics, and robust software engineering for edge compute platforms.

    During my M.A.Sc. at the University of Waterloo, I worked with Dr. William Melek and Dr. George Shaker, and collaborated with Apple, UW RoboHub, Keysight, and Rogers on connected robotics and sensing systems.

    Earlier, I developed a strong interest in multi-robot systems and field robotics operations, including underwater inspection pipelines using sonar and ROV/AUV deployments, and multi-agent autonomy projects in academic settings.
